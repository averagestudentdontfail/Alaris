Detailed Description

Unified numerical integration facade that automatically selects optimal implementation. Component ID: STPR002C.

Dispatches to either:

  • STPR002B - AVX2-accelerated 32-point Gauss-Legendre (if AVX2 available)
  • STPR002A - MathNet.Numerics 128-point Gauss-Legendre (fallback)

Use this facade in hot paths for automatic hardware optimization.
