static

Integrates a real-valued function over a finite interval using high-order Gauss-Legendre quadrature.

Integrates from a to infinity using Adaptive Truncation with Gauss-Legendre. Instead of mapping (a, inf) -> (-1, 1), we integrate in chunks [a, a+step], [a+step, a+2*step]... until the contribution of a chunk is smaller than the tolerance. Uses adaptive step sizing: step doubles when convergence accelerates. This is superior for oscillatory Characteristic Functions (Heston/Kou).
