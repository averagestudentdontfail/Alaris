Detailed Description

Universe selection model that filters for symbols with upcoming earnings. Implements Atilgan (2014) pre-screening criteria.

Selection criteria (from Atilgan 2014):

  • Minimum 30-day average dollar volume: $1.5M
  • Minimum price: $5.00
  • Earnings announcement in T+[5,7] days
  • Options available with sufficient liquidity

The universe refreshes daily at market open, querying the earnings calendar provider for symbols with announcements in the target window.
