◆ STTM002AType

enum Alaris.Strategy.Core.STTM002AType

Defines earnings-related regimes for IV modeling.

Enumerator
NoEarnings 

No earnings event before option expiration. Use standard models (BSM, Heston, Kou).

PreEarnings 

Pre-earnings announcement regime. IV typically elevated due to anticipated jump. Use L&S model or jump-augmented models.

PostEarningsTransition 

Post-earnings announcement regime (0-5 days after). IV typically collapses as uncertainty resolves. Transitional period back to normal.

PostEarningsNormal 

Normal regime after earnings effects dissipate. Use standard continuous models.
