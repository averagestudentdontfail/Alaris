Detailed Description

Generates trading signals for earnings calendar spread opportunities. Implements the strategy from Atilgan (2014) and incorporates term structure analysis. Enhanced with Leung & Santoli (2014) model for theoretical IV and mispricing signals.

Calculates Leung & Santoli (2014) model metrics including:

  • Earnings jump volatility (sigma_e) calibrated from historical EA moves
  • Theoretical pre-EA implied volatility: I(t) = sqrt(sigma^2 + sigma_e^2/(T-t))
  • IV mispricing signal (market IV vs theoretical)
  • Expected IV crush magnitude
